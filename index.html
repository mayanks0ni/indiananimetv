    All Commands Listed Below -

    User Info Commands

    avatar - To View The Avatar Of Yourself Or The Mentioned User!

    userinfo - To Get The UserInfo Of Yourself Or The Mentioned User!

    serverinfo - To Get The Info Of The Server!

    Moderation Commands

    kick - A Command To Kick The Mentioned User! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    ban - A Command To Ban The Mentioned User! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    announce - A Command To Announce A Message In The Channel In Which The Command Is Used Or The 1st Mentioned Channel! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    del - A Command To Delete Messages By Specifying The Number! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    dm - A Command To Send The Mentioned User A Direct Message Through The Bot! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    mentionrolee - A Command To Mention The Role Through The Bo By Specifying Its Name! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    role - A Command To Add A Particular Role To The Mentioned User! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    takerole - A Command To Take A Particular Role From The Mentioned User! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    setnick - A Command To Set The Nickname Of The Mentioned User! [Accessible by - Staffs & Mods]

    suggest - A Command To Make Suggestions! [Accessible by - Members]
